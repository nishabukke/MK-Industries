Our Commitment to Innovation: The M & K Industries Way

M&K Industries, established in 2007, is a leading provider of heavy steel fabrication, machining, assembly of steel structures, Fuel Handling equipment, and industrial process equipment for various industries such as Cement, Sugar, Power, Chemical, Metallurgical, Fertilizer, Petrochemical, Oil & Gas, Steel, Mining, and more. The company specializes in Heavy Structural Fabrication, Fabricated Fuel Handling equipment’s, Industrial Special Purpose Products for Boiler and Process Industry and Sugar related Heavy Fabrication equipment’s.

The company has three modern manufacturing facilities spread over 8 acres with three workshops, a monthly production capacity of 300 MT, and a workforce of around 150 employees. The facilities are equipped with modern machinery and equipment to maximize automation. M&K Industries prides itself on quality and delivery, and has a national network of reliable vendors to ensure smooth progress of work on the shop floor. The company has been certified as an ISO 9001:2015 compliant organization.

M&K Industries has a strong client base, including leading global companies such as Thermax Limited, ThyssenKrupp India Limited, Zetwerk, AYOKI , Roquette Riddhi Siddhi , Schenck Process ,Shree Renuka Sugars, Yashwant Iron, Nuclear Power Corporation of India and many more. The company follows international codes and standards recommended by its customers, such as ASME, BS, DIN, IS, and others.

It is our pride to be recognized as Self-Certified vendors for major customers.

We are dedicated to purpose, progress, and integrity.
